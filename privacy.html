Yog Philosphy – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Yog Philosphy (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website or automation workflows that integrate with TikTok (the “Service”).

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account and use Yog Philosphy, we may collect:

  • TikTok OAuth tokens (access and refresh tokens)
  • TikTok Open ID and basic profile information allowed by the scopes you approve
  • Video files, captions, and metadata you choose to upload through the Service

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you with TikTok via TikTok Login Kit
  • Upload and publish or draft videos to your TikTok account through TikTok’s Content Posting API
  • Check and display upload or processing status to you

We do not use your TikTok data for advertising, profiling, or purposes unrelated to providing the Service.

4. Legal Basis

We process your information based on your explicit consent when you authorize Yog Philosphy to access your TikTok account and on our legitimate interest in operating and improving the Service.

5. Data Storage and Security

TikTok tokens and related credentials are stored securely (for example, in encrypted n8n credentials) and are only accessible to the systems required to operate the Service. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share your data only with TikTok as necessary to provide the Service (e.g., when authenticating you or uploading videos via TikTok’s APIs), and as required by law.

7. Data Retention

We retain your TikTok tokens and related data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service or as required by law. If you revoke access to Yog Philosphy from your TikTok account or request deletion, we will remove or anonymize associated data within a reasonable time.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Revoke Yog Philosphy’s access to your TikTok account at any time in TikTok settings
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we hold about you
  • Contact us with any questions or privacy concerns

9. Third‑Party Services

Yog Philosphy uses TikTok’s official APIs and developer tools. Your use of TikTok is also governed by TikTok’s Privacy Policy and other TikTok terms and policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Your continued use of Yog Philosphy after changes take effect means you accept the revised Policy.
