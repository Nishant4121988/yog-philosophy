About Yog Philosophy

Yog Philosophy is a dedicated platform for exploring and sharing the profound wisdom of Yogic traditions. Our mission is to make spiritual teachings accessible to everyone — through high-quality video content published directly to TikTok.

We empower spiritual content creators to automate their publishing workflow without compromising authenticity. Whether you are a Yoga teacher, a philosophy enthusiast, or a spiritual educator, Yog Philosophy is your trusted publishing companion.

Our platform integrates with TikTok for Developers to provide a seamless, secure, and efficient content publishing experience.

TikTok Integration

Yog Philosophy uses TikTok's official developer platform to enable the following capabilities:

  • TikTok Login Kit — Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with your TikTok account
  • Content Posting API — Upload and publish videos directly to your TikTok profile
  • User Info API — Access basic profile data (open_id, display name, avatar) to personalise your experience
  • Sandbox Testing — All integrations are first tested in TikTok Sandbox before going live

Yog Philosophy complies with all TikTok Platform Policies. We do not misuse API access or user data.
