TikTok Integration

Yog Philosophy uses TikTok's official Content Posting API and Login Kit to securely authenticate users and publish videos on their behalf.

We request only the minimum permissions needed: user.info.basic, video.upload, and video.publish.

Permissions Requested

  • ✅ user.info.basic — Read your basic profile info (name, avatar)
  • ✅ video.upload — Upload videos to your TikTok account
  • ✅ video.publish — Publish uploaded videos to your profile
